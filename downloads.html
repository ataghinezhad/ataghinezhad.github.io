Advance Database

An advanced database course is a higher-level study of database systems. It typically covers a variety of topics including but not limited to: 1) Query Optimization: Techniques to improve the efficiency of data retrieval. 2) Concurrency: Managing simultaneous operations. 3) CAP Theorem: Understanding the tradeoffs between data consistency, availability, and resilience.
